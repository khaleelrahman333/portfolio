About Me

I’m a comedy writer and audio producer that’s written for online outlets like The Onion, Reductress, Slackjaw, and Widget Magazine. In 2024, I co-founded a humor brand called The Musk satirizing men’s media.

I’ve also worked as a talk show producer for Audacious with Chion Wolf on Connecticut Public Radio.
